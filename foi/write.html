What do you want to know?

Clear, simple requests help our staff understand what you need.

Tips

  • Keep it focused: you’re more likely to get what you want
  • Write in plain English: it’ll help our staff understand what you’re looking for
  • It helps if you define the specific time period you’re interested in
  • If you want information about yourself, make a Subject Access request instead
